No. Ð unique stem identifierLianaID Ð unique individual identifierStemID Ð unique identifier for multiple stems within an individualCensus Ð the census number: 1 = 2007; 2 = 2017PX Ð x coordinate of stem within the plotPY Ð y coordinate of stem within the plotDBH Ð stem diameter at breast height in millimetersStem Ð the type of stem: P = principle stem; C = clonal stem; B = branchStatus Ð the status of the stem: L = living/persistent; N = new recruit; D = dead; A = absent Ð a placeholder in older census for stems that recruited in the future census.
